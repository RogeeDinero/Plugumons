⚡ Staking Documentation

Everything you need to know about Plugumons Energy Grid

🎯 Staking Overview

Stake your PLUGU tokens to earn rewards and power up the Energy Grid! Choose from multiple lock periods with different APR rates. The longer you stake, the higher your rewards!

🎁 NFT Holder Benefits

Who qualifies? Anyone holding a Plugumons NFT (Collection: 4Qy6grGLpMBk2q13tPt32UkCzahWCSLEBLbRvHCZYket)

✨ Special Advantages:

  • ✅ No Lock Period - Unstake anytime, no waiting!
  • ✅ 2x Multiplier - After reaching halfway point of lock period
  • ✅ Higher APR - Earn double the standard rate

How the 2x Multiplier Works:
• 30-Day stake → 2x APR kicks in after 15 days (5% becomes 10%)
• 90-Day stake → 2x APR kicks in after 45 days (10% becomes 20%)
• 365-Day stake → 2x APR kicks in after 182 days (20% becomes 40%)

⚡ Standard Staking (Non-NFT Holders)

Standard users earn rewards with locked staking periods.

📋 Staking Rules:

  • 🔒 Tokens are LOCKED - Cannot unstake before lock period ends
  • ⏰ Choose Your Period - 30, 90, or 365 days
  • 📈 Fixed APR - Rate guaranteed for entire period
  • 💰 Claim Anytime - Claim rewards while staking
30 Days: 5% Fixed APR
90 Days: 10% Fixed APR
365 Days: 20% Fixed APR

⚡ Total Grid Power

Community Power Boost! When the community stakes 200,000,000 PLUGU tokens, the grid reaches 100% charge and ALL stakers (NFT and non-NFT) get 2x APR BOOST!

🔋 Grid Boost Benefits:

  • ⚡ All APRs Double - Everyone earns 2x rewards!
  • 🎁 Stacks with NFT Bonuses - NFT holders can earn up to 80% APR!
  • 🔄 Always Active at 100% - Boost remains as long as grid is charged

Example with Grid Boost:
• Standard 365-day: 20% → 40% APR
• NFT 365-day (after halfway): 40% → 80% APR

🏭 Your Power Stations

Each stake you create becomes a "Power Station" that generates rewards over time.

  • View All Stakes - See each power station with real-time rewards
  • Track Progress - Monitor days remaining and percentage complete
  • Claim Rewards - Collect your earned PLUGU anytime
  • Unstake - Withdraw when lock period ends (or anytime for NFT holders)

🏆 Top Power Plants

The leaderboard shows the top 5 stakers by total staked amount. Compete with the community to see who powers the grid the most!

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack)
  2. Use the Reward Calculator to estimate your earnings
  3. Choose your staking period (30, 90, or 365 days)
  4. Click "Start Staking" and confirm the transaction
  5. Watch your Power Station generate rewards!
